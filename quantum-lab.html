Quantum-Enhanced Intuition

Placeholder text explaining the lab's vision. While classical computers are powerful, some problems involving vast combinatorial spaces are fundamentally intractable. Quantum computers excel at these. The Quantum Lab's mission is to identify which components of our Causal AI can be offloaded to a quantum co-processor (QPU). The primary candidate is the QCIA, where a QPU could allow the AI to hold multiple causal hypotheses in superposition, providing a powerful form of computational intuition.
